## Purpose

This annex is reserved for future discussion of **governance models**
applicable to UCPIS stewardship in contexts of real-world adoption.

Governance is treated as a downstream concern, activated only when
multiple stakeholders are actively using or extending the architecture.

---

## Publication Criteria

This annex will be authored and published **only after** one or more of the
following conditions are met:

- Sustained external adoption of UCPIS concepts
- Participation by multiple independent organizations
- Explicit delegation of stewardship responsibilities

Governance models are not defined in advance of adoption.

---

## Scope (When Published)

When activated, this annex may describe:

- Stewardship structures
- Change control processes
- Transparency and accountability principles
- Anti-capture mechanisms
- Public-interest alignment

No governance authority is asserted by this work.

---

## Current Status

No governance model is currently defined.

This annex is intentionally deferred to prevent premature institutionalization.
